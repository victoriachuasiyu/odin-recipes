Tofu Meat

Mummy's signature tofu meat...what is that??

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 blocks of soft tofu
  • 1 packet of minced meat
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of pepper
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp of cornstarch

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the minced meat with soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, pepper, garlic, and cornstarch until well combined.
  2. Add oil and garlic to pan
  3. Add meat to pan
  4. Add tofu to pan
