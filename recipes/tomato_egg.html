Tomato Egg

Austin's feelings-based tomato egg...quesionable taste.

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp ketchup
  • 15ml sesame oil
  • 10ml vegetable oil
  • 7 tbsp water

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl and add salt, pepper, and water. Mix well.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and cook until the eggs are set but still slightly runny. Remove the eggs from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add a little more oil if needed and sauté the chopped tomatoes until they are soft and release their juices.
  4. Add water to the pan while sautéing the tomatoes.
  5. Return the cooked eggs to the pan with the tomatoes and gently stir to combine.
  6. Add oyster sauce and ketchup to the pan, stirring gently to coat the eggs and tomatoes with the sauce.
  7. Cook for another minute until everything is heated through.
  8. Drizzle sesame oil over the dish and give it a final stir.
  9. Serve hot with rice or as a side dish.
