Curry Chicken

Most Delicious Curry Chicken made with curry, chicken and mom's touch.

Ingredients

  • 2kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté the onions until golden brown.
  2. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. Add the curry powder and cook for 30 seconds.
  4. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are no longer pink.
  5. Pour in the coconut milk and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat and let it simmer for 30 minutes or until the chicken is tender.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
